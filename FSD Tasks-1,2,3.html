AI Fruits

AI fruit videos have become a massive viral phenomenon across TikTok and Instagram Reels. This trend features anthropomorphic fruit characters with human faces, eyes, and voices engaging in highly dramatic, soap-opera-style storylines. Often referred to as "fruit slop" or "AI slop," these videos generate millions of views by placing cute produce in absurdly tense human situations.
